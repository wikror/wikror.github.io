Individuality in Biology and Cognitive Sciences

The goal of the project is to investigate the notion of biological individuality at the intersection of philosophy of biology and philosophy of mind and of cognitive science. Individuality poses currently a significant problem for contemporary theories of life, and there are many attempts to provide a definition for this concept.
